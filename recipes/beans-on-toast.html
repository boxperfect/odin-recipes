Beans On Toast


Few words for introduction

The ultimate speedy dinner. (Or lunch, or breakfast...)

Ingredients

  • 4 slices Co-op farmhouse loaf
  • 1 x 420g Co-op baked beans
  • 60g Co-op mature Cheddar cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat the beans just like it says on the tin
  2. Spoon over hot buttered toast and sprinkle with plenty of grated cheese
  3. Make sure your dinner is fully cooked and piping hot before serving it up
